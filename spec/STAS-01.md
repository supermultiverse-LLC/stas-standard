# STAS-01

Shared Taproot Assets Standard

Status: Draft  
Category: Standards Track

## Abstract

STAS-01 defines the normative representation of Bitcoin Digital Objects.

This specification establishes the requirements that conforming
implementations must follow to represent, identify, exchange, and verify
Bitcoin Digital Objects interoperably.

STAS-01 evolves through the governance and RFC processes defined by the
STAS project.
